# UAE utility tariffs (reference)

This document is a short reference for common UAE utility providers and tariff types. It is for educational use only; always confirm with the provider.

## Providers

| Provider | Coverage | Typical use |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| **DEWA** | Dubai | Electricity, water |
| **FEWA** | Northern Emirates (Ajman, UAQ, RAK, Fujairah) | Electricity, water |
| **ADDC** | Abu Dhabi (city) | Electricity, water |
| **SEWA** | Sharjah | Electricity, water |
| **Empower** | Dubai (district cooling) | District cooling |
| **Tabreed** | Abu Dhabi / national | District cooling |
| **Du / Etisalat** | UAE-wide | Internet, telecoms |

## Tariff types (typical)

- **Slab rates:** Consumption bands with different AED per unit (e.g. first 2000 gal at X, next at Y).
- **Time-of-use:** Peak vs off-peak rates (where applicable).
- **Fixed charges:** Monthly connection or demand charges.

## Units

- Electricity: **kWh**
- Water: **Imperial gallons** (often reported in 1000s)
- District cooling: **Refrigeration ton (RT)** or **kWh** depending on provider

## Disclaimer

Rates and structures change. This is not official advice. Check each provider’s current tariff documents.
